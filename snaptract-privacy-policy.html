Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

01What we collect

Your profile (name, currency, and any employee/department details you add), the receipts and reports you create, and — if you enable notifications — a device token used to deliver them.

02How we use it

Only to run the app for you: reading receipts with AI, syncing your data across your devices, showing your spending, and sending notifications you've allowed.

03Where it's stored

Your data is stored in our Supabase database, scoped so only you can read it; receipt images are stored in Cloudflare R2. Some processing happens on your device.

04Third parties

We use Apple (sign-in, subscriptions, push), an AI provider to read receipts, Supabase (database & functions), and Cloudflare (image storage). We don't sell your data or use it for advertising.

05Your choices

You can edit or delete your data, or delete your entire account, from Profile at any time. Deleting your account permanently removes your data.

06Retention

We keep your data until you delete it or close your account.

07Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us through Contact us in the app, or email support@snaptract.app.
