Privacy Policy

Falaah is a Muslim prayer companion. Your worship is between you and Allah — this policy explains what we collect, what we never do, and the choices that stay yours.

1Our commitment

Falaah is built to protect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

2Information we collect

  • Account: your Apple ID identifier and email address via Sign in with Apple (you may choose to hide your email).
  • Worship data you create: prayer logs, qaza (make-up) balances, goals, dhikr counts, memorization progress, and reflections.
  • Recitation recordings, only if you choose to make them.
  • A device notification token, only if you enable reminders.

3What we do not collect

We do not collect your precise location — prayer times are calculated on your device and your location never leaves your phone. We do not use advertising identifiers, third-party analytics or tracking, and we never sell your data. Your qaza estimation answers stay on your device; only the resulting totals are saved.

4How we use your information

To provide and sync the app's features, back up your data when you sign in or enable backup, send the reminders you turn on, and respond to your support requests. We do not use your data for advertising.

5AI processing

When you use AI features, the text or audio you submit is sent to our AI processor (OpenAI) to generate a response. Under OpenAI's API terms, this content is not used to train their models. Please only submit what you are comfortable having processed.

6Where your data is stored

Structured data is stored on our managed backend (Supabase). Files you choose to back up, such as recordings, may be stored with a cloud object-storage provider (Cloudflare R2). These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms and security.

7Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We share it only with the service providers who help us operate the app (hosting, AI processing, and notification delivery), and where we are required to by law.

8Your rights & choices

You can view your data in the app, and permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Account & preferences. You can turn reminders on or off in Settings. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights to access, correct, or export your data — contact us to exercise them.

9Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your associated data from our systems, except where we are required to retain it by law.

10Children's privacy

Falaah is not directed to children under 13, or the age of digital consent where you live. We do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

11Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12International users

Your data may be processed in countries other than the one you live in. By using the app, you consent to this processing consistent with this policy.

13Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version, and material changes will be highlighted in the app.

14Contact

Privacy questions? Email support@mysalahtracker.com.
