Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Towly (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Towly mobile application (the “App”). By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information: the email address (and, for Sign in with Apple, the name you choose to share) used to create your account.
  • Location data: your device location, used to show nearby tow reports, warn you when you enter a saved place, and tag reports you submit. You can revoke location access at any time in your device settings.
  • Reports you submit: the location, incident type, notes, towing company name, and any photos you attach to a report.
  • Parking checks and saved places: the spots you rate and the places you choose to watch.

How we use your information

  • To operate the community map and warn drivers about predatory-tow areas.
  • To send you proximity alerts for places you save.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the App.

What is public

Tow reports and parking checks you submit are shared with the community on the map and feed. Your email address, saved places, and account identifiers are never shown to other users.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We use Supabase to host our database, authentication, and file storage. Report photos are stored privately and served through time-limited signed links.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete My Account inside the App. This permanently removes your account and private data (saved places, profile). Reports and parking checks you contributed may remain on the community map in anonymized form (no longer linked to your account). To request deletion by email, contact support@towly.app.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions? Email support@towly.app.
